Guess the number I'm thinking of!

Play the number-guessing game.

The rules:

  • On the first level you start guessing the numbers between 1 and 10.
  • You have 3 guesses.
  • If your guess is correct, you get one point and move to the next level.
  • On every subsequent level the interval of the numbers is increased by 10.
  • If your guess is incorrect, we give you a hint, whether the correct number is greater or smaller than you guessed.
  • You can see the numbers you already guessed on this level.
  • If you guess incorrectly three times in a row, the game is over.
  • But you can always start over by clicking the button "Reset"
